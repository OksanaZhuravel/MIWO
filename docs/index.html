About MIWO Project

MIWO service for foreigners and migrant workers is coming soon! Join us today to be one of the first to experience the family financial support digital service. It will be available instantly as we get a license in the United Arab Emirates. The service give you freedom to provide for your family in the home country easy, remote and instant access to money anytime (24/7) and worldwide.

The service is pure digital. It means our all in one mobile application allows you to get a local account (in AED), make instant cross border money transfers using any currency (Rs, Tk, PKR, EGP, USD, etc.), manage any payments and transactions 100% online. No need to visit a bank branch or an office of money transfer service to you or your relatives (money recipients) anymore as virtual bank cards is available instantly in any country, so you don’t need to send them or worry about loss or fraud. The virtual card can be used to make any bank transactions in the same way as a usual physical debit or credit card is. It’s available to pay in shops and stores, making digital payments and even remunerating for online subscription services. Virtual cards can also be used with ApplePay, AndroidPay, SamsungPay and similar services.
